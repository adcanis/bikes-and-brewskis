We know what you're probably thinking...

These people can't really just ride around, drinking beer can they?

As much fun as that sounds, no we don't. At Bikes and Brewskis, we are much more than some enthusiasts who enjoy a cold one.

Without our trails we wouldn't have as many fun and exciting places to ride. Our community events aim to promote responsible enjoyment for everyone.

Hitting the trails is fun. But with so many small towns making up this great county, we know we can do better. That's why we are working tiredlessly to help create greener town centres with real bike lanes and accessible wheels for everyone.

We aren't dubbed Ontario's Garden for no reason and our local restaurants know it. So when we're not out taking down some miles and throwing back a beer, we're sticking to our "greener, healthier" slogan and helping our small businesses get their food out to their customers on two wheels!
